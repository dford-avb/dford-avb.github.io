Getting the PHP 5.4 patch for Openmage Enterprise Edition (EE) and Community Edition (CE)

Openmage has a patch that enables you to use PHP 5.4.x with Openmage Enterprise Edition (EE) and Openmage Community Edition (CE).

This patch applies to:

  • Openmage CE versions 1.6.0.0–1.8.1.0
  • Openmage EE versions 1.11.0.0–1.13.1.0

To get the patch for Openmage CE:

  1. Go to www.openmagecommerce.com/download.
  2. Click My Account in the upper right corner of the page.
  3. Log in when prompted.
  4. Scroll to the Openmage Community Edition Patches section.
  5. Follow the prompts on your screen to get the PHP 5.4 support patch.

To get the patch for Openmage EE:

  1. Log in to www.openmagecommerce.com.
  2. In the left pane, click Downloads.
  3. In the right pane, click Openmage Enterprise Edition.
  4. Follow the prompts on your screen to download the PHP 5.4 Compatibility patch.
  5. Apply the patch as discussed in How to Apply and Revert Openmage Patches.
