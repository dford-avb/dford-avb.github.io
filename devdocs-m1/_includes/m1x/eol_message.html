Openmage 1.x Software Support Notice

For Openmage Commerce 1, Openmage is providing software support through June 2020. Depending on your Openmage Commerce 1 version, software support may include both quality fixes and security patches. Please review our Openmage Software Lifecycle Policy to see how your version of Openmage Commerce 1 is supported.

For Openmage Open Source 1.5 to 1.9, Openmage is providing software security patches through June 2020 to ensure those sites remain secure and compliant. Visit our information page for more details about our software maintenance policy and other considerations for your business.
